2015

The Birth of Montage Inc. an Edit, LLC Company

After completing a decade of custom integration projects for everyone from the Department of Defense to Yahoo Sports, cofounder Irma Olguin, JR. started noticing patterns; e.g., while the noun “Inventory Data” might be replaced with another noun, “Marketing Data,” the driving need to combine them into one common cloud-based platform had largely the same criteria. This problem wasn’t isolated, development teams large and small allocate a huge percentage of resources to writing custom integration code to combine multiple silos of data. There had to be a better way. In typical innovative fashion, she sought out to create it. Again joining forces with Brad Gleason who brings over 25 years experience in planning and executing management strategies for farming operations of all sizes; they set to transform the way agriculture and other industries address data integration needs, and Montage was born.

2013

Irma cofounds Bitwise Industries

Bitwise is comprised of 4 divisions: Geekwise Academy, a training program that teaches real-world technology skills at a super-fast pace; Hashtag, a collaborative workspace; Shift3 Technologies, a software development and infrastructure firm; and a building division providing world-class working environments that engage and inspire and house more than 40 technology companies.

2012

Irma & Brad cofound Edit, LLC

Brad and Irma joined forces to launch Edit, LLC with the mission to make real products for real people. Edit, LLC is a forward thinking software company that developed the drag-and-drop website builder Buildicus and the innovative Buildicus Concierge services, which pairs just out of design school interns with seasoned developers to deliver cutting edge professional websites at a fraction of the cost of alternatives.

Irma launches fundraising platforms Overfundit

OverFundIt provides a platform for Doers who want to raise funds and change the current reality within their community. Red tape is replaced with real results.

Brad cofounds Nature's Joy

Nature’s Joy markets the highest quality California pistachios to meet the increasing demand for healthy snacks with the goal of creating a seamless farm to store experience for food retailers across the United States.
